One home for
everything you travel with.

UniProfile gives travelers a single private place for their documents, trips, insurance, preferences, and family — and is being built to eventually replace the dozens of forms you fill out at every booking. A lot of that works today. Here's the honest split before you sign up.

Working today
Document vault & expiry alerts
Passport, visa, KTN, Global Entry, ESTA, vaccination records — encrypted in one place. We watch expiry dates against your trips and warn you before it's a problem.
Trip vault by email
Forward any booking confirmation — flights, hotels, trains, cruises — and it's parsed and saved in one place, searchable by destination and date.
Family & companion profiles
Track your parents', kids', and partner's documents alongside yours. Expiry alerts cover the whole household, not just you.
Preferences, saved once
Seat, meal, room type, bed firmness, loyalty numbers — written down once in your profile instead of re-entered at every site.
Insurance & tours
Store travel insurance policies and booked tours or activities alongside your trips. Attach PDFs, track coverage dates, and see what's live versus upcoming.
Group bookings
Manage group travel in one place — shared manifests, per-member document status, and group notes, all without a spreadsheet.
Cruise profile
Cabin preferences, dining seatings, onboard packages, and shore excursion notes — a dedicated section for cruisers.
Consent ledger & data rights
Every data-sharing event is logged with who saw what and when. Revoke any operator's access instantly. Full GDPR export on demand.
Coming soon
Autofill at airline.com
The 25 to 40 fields you type at every checkout — name, passport, KTN, frequent flyer — will pull from your profile in one click. Browser extension coming.
Push notifications
Right now, alerts come by email. Mobile apps with push, biometric unlock, and offline document access are next.
Apple & Google Wallet
Boarding passes and verified travel credentials in your phone wallet, signed by UniProfile.
Airlines that recognize you
Direct integrations so your KTN, status, and preferences arrive at the airline before you do — no operator-side data loss.
Visa & entry-rule checks
Real-time monitoring of visa validity, ETA expirations, and destination-rule changes against your itinerary.
Bleisure separation
When a work trip extends into a personal weekend, the policy, payment, and loyalty contexts switch automatically.
A few things worth knowing
  • We don't sell your data. Not to advertisers, not to anyone.
  • You can export everything and delete your account at any time. No lock-in, no dark patterns.
  • Every operator that touches your data is listed in your consent ledger. You can revoke access to any of them instantly.
  • This is early. You'll see rough edges. Tell us about them — that's the trade we're asking for.
