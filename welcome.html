Arrive ready. Stay in control.

UniProfile gives travelers a single private place for their documents, trips, insurance, preferences, and travel companions — and is being built to eventually replace the dozens of forms you fill out at every booking. A lot of that works today. Here's the honest split before you sign up.

Working today
Document vault & expiry alerts
Passport, visa, KTN, Global Entry, ESTA, driver's licence — in one place. We watch expiry dates against your trips and alert you before it becomes a problem.
Trip vault by email
Forward any booking confirmation — flights, hotels, trains, cruises — to your personal vault address. It's parsed and saved, searchable by destination and date.
Travel companions & family
Add named profiles for travel companions and family members, each with their own documents and expiry dates. Manage everyone's paperwork without juggling separate records.
Preferences, saved once
Seat position, cabin class, meal code, hotel bed type, loyalty numbers — written down once in your profile. The same list every booking asks you to retype.
Travel insurance
Store your travel insurance policy — provider, coverage dates, and emergency phone number — alongside your passport and other documents.
Journeys
Create a Journey for any trip — solo it's your own complete record; with others, everyone shares one readiness view. Document status, itinerary, and emergency contacts without a spreadsheet.
Cruise profile
Cabin preference, deck level, dining seating, excursion style, sailing type — saved once, available for every booking.
Coming soon
Autofill at airline.com
The 25 to 40 fields you type at every checkout — name, passport, KTN, frequent flyer — will pull from your profile in one click. Browser extension coming.
Push notifications
Right now, alerts come by email. Mobile apps with push, biometric unlock, and offline document access are next.
Apple & Google Wallet
Boarding passes and verified travel credentials in your phone wallet, signed by UniProfile.
Airlines that recognize you
Direct integrations so your KTN, status, and preferences arrive at the airline before you do — no operator-side data loss.
Visa & entry-rule checks
Basic entry-rule checks run today in Journey readiness — passport validity against your destination. Real-time monitoring of ETA expirations and destination-rule changes is coming.
Bleisure separation
When a work trip extends into a personal weekend, the policy, payment, and loyalty contexts switch automatically.
Consent ledger & data rights
A complete audit trail of who has seen your data and when — with per-operator access revocation and data export. Today you can view your recent account activity in Privacy; the full consent ledger is in development.
A few things worth knowing
  • We don't sell your data. Not to advertisers, not to anyone.
  • You can delete your account at any time. No lock-in, no dark patterns.
  • This is early. You'll see rough edges. Tell us about them — that's the trade we're asking for.
