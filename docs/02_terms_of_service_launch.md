## 1. About UniProfile

UniProfile is a traveler-sovereign identity platform. We provide tools that allow you to store and manage your travel-related identity, documents, preferences, loyalty memberships, payment instruments, and consent grants in one place, and to share that information with travel operators (airlines, hotels, cruise lines, rail operators, online travel agencies, travel management companies, and similar) on your terms.

We are not a travel agent, a tour operator, an airline, or a booking service. We do not search for, sell, or process travel reservations. We do not hold travel inventory. We supply identity and consent infrastructure to systems that do.

You retain ownership of all data you provide to UniProfile. We act as a custodian, not an owner.

Journeys (user-created trip groups between UniProfile members) are a separate, live feature governed by the consent provisions in §4, not by this section.

### 5.1 What "connected operators" means

When you grant a travel operator (airline, hotel, OTA, TMC, etc.) access to specified parts of your UniProfile, you create a "Connected Operator" relationship. The operator may receive only the data scoped in your consent grant, only for the duration you specified, and only for the purposes you authorized.

### 5.2 Operator agreements are separate

When you book travel through a Connected Operator, your relationship with that operator is governed by the operator's own terms, conditions, and privacy policies. UniProfile is not party to your booking agreement, your transport contract, your accommodation contract, or any other arrangement between you and the operator. Disputes about flights, hotel stays, cruises, or any other travel service must be resolved with the relevant operator.

### 5.3 What operators may do with your data

Operators are bound by their own data protection obligations and the consent terms you grant through UniProfile. If you believe an operator has violated those terms, contact UniProfile and we will investigate, including the option to revoke that operator's access.

### 5.4 Browser extension

> ⚠ **NOT YET LIVE AT LAUNCH:** The browser extension described in this section does not exist at launch.

If you install the UniProfile browser extension, the extension operates on the websites you visit, subject to:

- Browser-managed permissions you grant at install time
