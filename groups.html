Groups

Travel together.

Soon.

Groups lets you bring people into your trips — family, a partner, colleagues on the same itinerary. Everyone in the group sees the same flights, hotels, and transfers as they come in. No forwarding PDFs, no syncing spreadsheets.

Before departure, UniProfile runs a pre-flight check for the whole group: passport expiry dates, visa requirements for each destination, seat gaps. If something doesn't add up for one person, it surfaces before anyone reaches the airport.

Add people by UniProfile number or by email. They receive an invitation, accept it, and they're in. The same privacy model applies — you decide what the group can see, and each member controls their own data.

Groups is coming in the next release.
