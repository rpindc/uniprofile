How it works

From UUID to Boarding Gate

Five steps, zero friction. The passenger profile travels with them across every system.

1
UUID Issuance
Passenger registers. UUID v7 issued instantly. Bleisure dual-context included. Biometrics enrolled.
2
Consent Granted
Passenger grants GDPR-compliant consent to airlines, TMCs, and airports. Granular and revocable.
3
Offer Personalised
NDC offers enriched with UUID preferences. Seat, meal, lounge, cabin auto-applied at booking.
4
Journey Tracked
Every touchpoint — check-in, boarding, baggage, disruption — resolved to the UUID in real time.
5
Intelligence Grows
ML learns preferences over time. Loyalty consolidated. Profile gets smarter with every trip.
