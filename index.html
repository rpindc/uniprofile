Our story
Built for the IATA OneOrder era.

UniProfile was founded on one belief: the passenger should own their identity, and that identity should travel seamlessly across every airline, TMC, and border in the world.

Just as Visa created a universal payment layer that works across every bank and merchant, UniProfile creates a universal identity layer that works across every airline, GDS, TMC, and airport — without any single entity owning the passenger.

🛂
Passenger First
Every consent is explicit, granular, and revocable. GDPR Article 7 is the floor, not the ceiling. The passenger owns their data — always.
🔗
Open Standards
Built on IATA OneOrder, NDC, ICAO 9303, and W3C WebAuthn. UniProfile contributes to open standards rather than creating proprietary lock-in.
⚡
Chaos Resilience
Our disruption engine uses chaos theory principles — the passenger UUID is the strange attractor around which every recovery orbit resolves.
🌍
Global by Design
Multi-AZ AWS infrastructure, GDPR + CCPA + PDPA compliance, and support for every IATA member airline from day one.
