Terms & privacy

At a glance

A plain-English summary of how UniProfile works, what we hold, and what you can expect from us.

The detailed legal documents — Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, Cookie Policy — are in review and will be published here when finalized. Until then, this page is the honest summary.

Your data is yours

You own what you upload to UniProfile — your passport details, visa data, trips, loyalty numbers, everything. We hold it as a custodian, not an owner.

What you can do today:

  • Revoke trip-group sharing — if you've shared a trip with companions, you can pull that sharing back from within the trip.
  • Request a data export, account deletion, or full revocation by writing to hello@uniprofile.net. We'll handle it personally — at our current stage, every request is read and answered by a person.
In active development

Self-serve data export, self-serve account deletion, and a full consent ledger with per-operator revocation are being built — they'll move from "email request" to "one-tap action" when they ship. Until then, our promise is that any request gets handled, not that you can do it in two clicks.

What we collect

Only what's needed to make UniProfile work for you.

  • Identity and travel documents you choose to upload (passport, visas, driver's license, loyalty, insurance, trusted-traveler programs).
  • Trip information you add or that we parse from emails you forward to us.
  • Account activity — when you log in, what consent you've given, what's been viewed — so you can see it yourself in your Privacy tab.
  • Technical basics — things like a session token that keeps you logged in.

That's the list. We don't collect browsing history, location tracking, contact lists, or anything else not on it.

What we don't do

Sometimes the clearest picture of a product is what it isn't.

  • We don't sell your data. Not to airlines, not to advertisers, not to data brokers, not to anyone.
  • We don't run ads. There are no advertisers paying for placement in UniProfile.
  • We don't have third-party tracking pixels. No Google Analytics, no Facebook pixel, no behavioral-tracking scripts on this site. You can verify it in your browser.
  • We don't share with airlines, hotels, or any third party unless you specifically and explicitly ask us to — for a purpose you can see, with a record of your consent.
  • We don't pretend to verify documents we haven't. What you give us is on file. We don't endorse it as authentic. When verified credentials become available, you'll see them marked as such.

Copyright and ownership

A clear line: your content stays yours, our product stays ours.

  • Your content — the documents you upload, the trips you record, the notes you write — is yours. UniProfile gets a limited right to store and process it only so we can provide the service you've asked for. We don't claim ownership, and we don't license your content to anyone else.
  • The UniProfile product — the name, design, code, brand, and "Arrive ready. Stay in control." — is ours. You're welcome to use UniProfile; you're not licensed to copy or rebrand it.

Who can use UniProfile

UniProfile is currently invitation-only. We onboard travelers carefully and intentionally, not in bulk.

You need to be 18 or older to use UniProfile. If you're under 18, this product isn't for you yet.

UniProfile is provided for personal travel-identity management. It's not for storing data on people who haven't consented to be in it, not for commercial document verification by third parties, and not for any of the abusive uses you'd expect a polite footnote to list.

What we can and can't promise

We work hard to keep your data safe and the service reliable. Your data is encrypted at rest using AWS-managed keys and in transit using HTTPS. Access to the systems holding your data is restricted to the small team that operates the service.

What we can't honestly promise:

  • That UniProfile will never have a bug.
  • That the service will never be unavailable for maintenance.
  • That we can guarantee any particular outcome at a border or with an airline. (Final decisions there rest with the relevant authorities — we say so explicitly wherever it matters.)

By using UniProfile, you're accepting the service as it is — useful, carefully built, but not magic. The full Terms of Service (publishing when legal review is done) covers liability and dispute resolution in proper legal language. Until then, this paragraph is our honest plain-language version.

How to reach us

If something on this page is unclear, if you've spotted a gap between what we say and what we do, or if you have a question about how your data is handled — write to us through our feedback page, or directly at hello@uniprofile.net.

We read what comes in. If a reply is needed, it comes within a few days.
